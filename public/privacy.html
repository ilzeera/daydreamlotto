Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

DayDream Lotto does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Dream descriptions and numbers you enter are sent to our server solely to generate your lottery numbers and are not stored or logged.

Local Storage

The app stores your reading history, Pro status, and daily usage count locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser or app data.

AI Processing

Dream descriptions are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI to generate lottery number suggestions. Please refer to Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com for details on how prompts are handled.

Entertainment Purpose

DayDream Lotto is for entertainment purposes only. No system can guarantee lottery wins. Please play responsibly.

Contact

For any privacy questions please contact us through the app's support channel.
